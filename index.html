About me

Namaste! I am Premprakash Khadka, a dedicated Surveyor at the Survey Department of Nepal. Originally from Tribeni Municipality, Rukum West, I am passionate about land surveying and using geospatial data to support Nepal's development. I completed my Diploma in Geomatics Engineering at the School of Geomatics and am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Geomatics Engineering at Kathmandu University.
